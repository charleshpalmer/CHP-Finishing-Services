Meet The Team!

We are always on the lookout for good staff, if you want a rewarding and challenging career and have the passion and skills to fit the criteria, we look forward to hearing from you. Contact us via Telephone or Email, or use the Contact Form on this website to get in touch with us!

Chris Palmer, Company Director.
Chris has occupied virtually every discipline regarding surface technology in a career spanning nearly 30 years, with a wealth of experience with both hands on craftsmanship backed by years of research and development and business management.

Ian our Chief Technician
Ian joined the company in 2008 and has helped to develop the company’s stronghold throughout the West Country. With Ian's vast wealth of interpersonal skills, product knowledge and a keen eye for detail you can be assured of the best results every time, Ian’s primary role as Chief Technician allows him to take on the most difficult repair with relative ease.
